nct-socialmedia-remoteDataMarkup

At the current time, a social media post is completely static and 
cannot update its information, such as a progress bar for a crowdfunding 
project.

A social media post might contain sports scores or a leaderboard and 
they would be able to receive updates because the post embedded template markup.  
Live updates would be fetched by the client while other kinds might 
be fetched by the website.

Posts with dynamic content allow embedding remote data sources, 
such as for a progress bar for a crowdfunding project that is always 
current.
