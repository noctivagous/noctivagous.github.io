Noctivagous Social Media

USER-LABELED INTENTIONS

On a different kind of social media website, when either a post or comment is made,
the user can categorize the intention with a label.  The post/comment
or comment will be color-coded with a label above it or carry other 
distinguishing features to indicate its purpose.

Benefits:
  

Here are some intention label categories:

Core
1. Description of Problem  (For posts, not comments)
2. Announcement  - (For posts, not comments)
3. Question – Seeking information or clarification
4. Answer / Help – Providing assistance or direct response
5. Friendly Amendment – Pointing out a potential error
6. Addition / Elaboration – Expanding on the topic.
7. Recommendation – Suggesting resources, tools, etc.

Potentially Controversial but Useful
10. Troll / Provocation – Self-identified trolling -- (Comments section only)
11. Rant – Long emotional outburst  -- (Comments section only, not posts)
12. Hot Take – Deliberately provocative opinion -- (Comments section only)

Niche or Context-Specific
13 Source Request – Asking for links/references
14 Translation – Providing or requesting one
15 Role-play / In-character – For fandom/gaming communities
16 Quote / Citation – Direct reference to something. Nothing
but the quote/ citation

Short Support
17 Shows Of Support Separated from The Like Button
