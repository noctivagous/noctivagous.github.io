
FOR AI CHATBOT SOFTWARE

-------

different types of clarifying questions.

    the user talks about a thing. the user then asks the ai
    to do a thing with the text provided or respond to it.
    
- understanding what the user wanted the ai to do in the request.

- understanding what the user was saying.


--------

for responses

a difference between

    - a conversational answer from the AI (can be put into the chat conversation).
        - the user has asked the AI what it can and cannot do or what it did, etc.
        - AI affirms or briefly answers a query.
    
    - the product of a task carried out by the AI (should be put into output pane).
        - code generated.
        - a translation made.
        - a research summary.
