Treble And Bass

Pressing a piano key on the left side of
a piano will produce a pitch only men
make in opera. For singing, the notes of
bass clef are associated with the
masculine, which reveals this yang
quality.  Male is associated with yang.
On the right side of the piano
are the high notes of treble clef that
are sung by women in opera, which
reveals this yin aspect. Yin is associated
with female. 

Yin and yang are not classifiers in a
superficial way, however. They are
properties found in all things in
multiple aspects.  We can see yin inside
yang and yang inside yin in this subject,
just like others. To be dark and low, and
more still, which is a lower frequency in
the sound wave, are yin characteristics.
To be bright and high, and more active, a
higher frequency in the sound wave, are
yang characteristics. In this way, the
interrelation and presence of yin and
yang is revealed in the discussion of
singing.
